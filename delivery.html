// The Fine Print

OUR POLICY

Simple, fair, and transparent.

⏱️
Delivery Times
Dakar: Same-day if ordered before 2PM, otherwise next-day.

Outside Dakar: Timing depends on your location. We confirm on WhatsApp.

Delivery times may vary during peak periods or public holidays.
💳
Payment on Delivery
For standard orders in Dakar, we offer cash on delivery. You pay when you receive the pair and have verified it.

For rare or limited pairs, or orders outside Dakar, a deposit may be required upfront to confirm the order.
📦
Packaging
All pairs are delivered in their original brand box, with all original accessories (laces, tags, dust bags where applicable).

We take care of every pair as if it were our own.
🔄
Exchange Policy
Size exchanges are accepted within 48 hours of delivery, provided the pair is in original unworn condition with the box.

If you receive a damaged or incorrect pair, contact us immediately — we will make it right, no questions asked.
