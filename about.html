// Our Story

WHO WE ARE

We are KICK DAKAR — a sneaker business born out of passion for rare, original pairs and a vision to bring exclusive footwear culture to Senegal.

// The Beginning

STARTED WITH A PASSION FOR KICKS

KICK DAKAR started as a simple idea: bring the world's most exclusive sneakers to Dakar. No fakes. No compromises. Just rare, original pairs at fair prices.

What began as a TikTok page sharing sneaker content quickly turned into a full business. With over 19,000 followers and hundreds of thousands of views, the demand was clear — Dakar wanted exclusive kicks.

Today we source directly from trusted suppliers around the world to bring you the most sought-after Nikes, Jordans, Adidas and more — delivered straight to your door.
