FAQ

Everything you need to know before placing your order. Can't find your answer? Message us on WhatsApp — we reply fast.

📱 Ordering
How do I place an order?+
Simply browse our catalog, click on the shoe you want, choose your size, and tap "Order on WhatsApp". This will open a WhatsApp chat with your order details pre-filled. Send the message and we'll confirm your order within minutes.
Do I need to create an account?+
No account needed. Everything happens through WhatsApp. Just click, choose your size, and message us. It's that simple.
Can I order multiple pairs at once?+
Yes! Just message us on WhatsApp with all the pairs you want and we'll put together one order for you. We can also offer better deals for bulk purchases.
What if the size I want is not available?+
Message us on WhatsApp with the shoe and size you're looking for. We can often source specific sizes on request, or put you on a waiting list for restocks.
✅ Authenticity
Are your sneakers 100% authentic?+
Absolutely. Every pair we sell is 100% original. We source from verified and trusted suppliers only. We have zero tolerance for fakes — our entire reputation is built on authenticity.
How can I verify my pair is authentic?+
All our pairs come with original boxes, tags, and laces. If you ever have doubts after receiving your pair, message us immediately — we will address it directly. You can also use apps like CheckCheck or Legit App to verify your pair.
Where do your sneakers come from?+
We source from trusted wholesale sneaker suppliers and verified resellers in key markets. All pairs go through our own quality check before being sold.
💰 Payment
What payment methods do you accept?+
We currently accept cash on delivery, Wave, Orange Money, and bank transfer. Payment details are confirmed via WhatsApp when you place your order.
Do I pay before or after delivery?+
For orders within Dakar we offer cash on delivery — you pay when you receive the pair. For orders outside Dakar or for rare/limited pairs, a deposit may be required to confirm your order.
Are the prices final or negotiable?+
Our prices are set fairly and reflect the market value of each pair. We do not negotiate on individual orders, but we offer deals on bulk purchases. Message us to discuss.
🔄 Exchanges & Returns
Can I exchange my pair if it doesn't fit?+
Yes, we offer size exchanges within 48 hours of delivery, as long as the pair is in its original, unworn condition with the box and all accessories. Message us immediately if there's a sizing issue.
What if I receive a damaged pair?+
This has never happened, but if it does — message us immediately with photos. We will resolve it fully, either with a replacement or a full refund. Your satisfaction is guaranteed.
Can I return a pair just because I changed my mind?+
We do not accept returns for change of mind once the pair has been delivered. However, if the pair is still in 100% original condition and has not been worn, contact us and we will see what we can do on a case-by-case basis.
